1. 수집하는 정보

  • 개발자는 개인정보를 직접 수집하지 않습니다. 알람·설정값은 기기 내부 저장소에만 보관됩니다.

  • 이 앱은 Google AdMob 광고를 표시합니다.

  • Google 개인정보처리방침: policies.google.com/privacy
  • 제어: 기기 설정 > Google > 광고에서 광고 ID를 재설정하거나 맞춤 광고를 해제할 수 있습니다.
  • 광고 제거 인앱 상품을 구매하면 배너·전면 광고가 표시되지 않습니다.

3. 제3자 제공

위(Google AdMob) 외의 제3자에게 제공하지 않습니다.

5. 보관·삭제

English

Mission Alarm (com.dksequence.missionalarm, by DKsequence) works without an account or sign-in. The developer does not directly collect or store your personal data.

1. Information we handle

  • The developer does not directly collect personal data. Alarms and settings are kept in the app's storage on your device.
  • They are removed when you uninstall the app.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

  • This app shows Google AdMob ads.
  • AdMob may collect device information such as the Advertising ID to serve and measure ads (per Google's policies).
  • Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalised ads in Settings > Google > Ads.
  • Purchasing the remove-ads in-app product stops banner and interstitial ads.

3. Third parties

No sharing with third parties other than Google AdMob as described above.

4. Children

The app is not directed at a specific age group, and the developer does not directly collect children's personal data.

5. Retention & deletion

Data stored on your device is deleted when the app is uninstalled.

English

This is an on-device news briefing app with no server. It does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. No accounts, ads, or analytics. Keywords, settings, and article metadata are stored on-device only. The app fetches public news RSS/web pages directly from your device using your keywords; no user identifiers are sent to third parties. Original articles open at the publisher's site in an in-app browser. Permissions: internet, notifications, optional battery-optimization exemption. Deleting the app removes all local data.

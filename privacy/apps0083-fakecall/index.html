1. Summary

Rescue Call has no accounts or login and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data (contact names, caller photos, reservations) is stored only on your device.

This app is a fake-call app for entertainment with no real calling capability. It never connects to a phone network and does not place or receive real calls.

2. What the app does NOT collect

  • Account info such as name, email, or phone number — never requested (no login).
  • The fake contact names and reservation times you enter — never sent to any server (local, on-device only).
  • A caller photo you choose — loaded from your gallery into the app only, never transmitted.

All data stays on your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

3. Permissions used

  • Notifications / Full-screen intent: to show the incoming-call screen over the lock screen at the time you scheduled.
  • Photo access (optional): only when you pick a caller photo; the chosen photo stays on your device.
  • Vibration: to vibrate during the incoming-call effect.

4. Third-party services (Ads & Purchases)

  • Google AdMob (ads): To keep the app free, ads are shown. AdMob may collect and process the Advertising ID and device information to serve and measure ads. A consent form (UMP) is shown to EEA/UK users. Purchasing "Premium" disables banner and interstitial ads (rewarded ads play only when the user chooses).
  • Google Play Billing: The "Rescue Call Premium" in-app purchase is processed by Google Play; the app does not store any payment information.

5. Children

This app is not directed to a specific age group and does not collect personal information.

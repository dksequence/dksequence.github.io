Translating

Digital twin spatial information translates the physical world into measurable, explorable digital space. This sequence focuses on location-based capture, spatial reconstruction, and the visual experience of data becoming place.

Description
This embedded viewer presents a digital twin scene as an interactive spatial archive. Instead of showing only a still result, it allows the viewer to move through the reconstructed space and experience how reality can be translated into data, geometry, and navigable visual information.
